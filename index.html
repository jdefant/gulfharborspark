Our Mission

The Gulf Harbors Neighborhood Park Organization is a non-profit dedicated to raising funds for the development of our community park. We aim to create a space where families can play, connect, and thrive, enhancing the quality of life in Gulf Harbors.

Our Park Project

A Vision for Gulf Harbors

Our goal is to transform the neighborhood park into a modern, inclusive space with playgrounds, walking paths, and green areas. Your donations will fund:

  • Safe, accessible playground equipment
  • Landscaping and tree planting
  • Benches and picnic areas
  • Lighting for evening use

Impact: Last year, our community raised $10,000, funding new swings. Help us reach $50,000 this year!
